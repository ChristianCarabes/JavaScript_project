Dragon ball Z

Dragon Ball Z follows the adventures of Goku, a Saiyan warrior, and his friends as they defend Earth from a variety of powerful villains. Set several years after the original Dragon Ball, Goku is now an adult and has settled down with his wife, Chi-Chi, and son, Gohan. The peace is disrupted when Goku’s extraterrestrial heritage is revealed by Raditz, his brother, who informs him that he is part of a nearly extinct race known as the Saiyans.

Evangelion

Neon Genesis Evangelion is a psychological mecha anime set in a post-apocalyptic world where humanity faces extinction from mysterious beings called "Angels." The story follows 14-year-old Shinji Ikari, who is recruited by his estranged father to pilot Evangelion, a biomechanical robot designed to fight the Angels. As Shinji and fellow pilots Asuka and Rei battle to protect Earth, they confront personal trauma, the complex motives of their superiors, and the nature of existence. The series delves into deep themes of identity, loneliness, and mental health, blending action with philosophical and psychological exploration.

Attack on titan

Attack on Titan is set in a world where humanity lives inside enormous walled cities to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures called Titans, who devour humans. The story follows Eren Yeager, his friends Mikasa and Armin, and the military forces fighting to uncover the truth behind the Titans and protect humanity. Eren vows to eliminate all Titans after one destroys his home and kills his mother. As the series unfolds, dark secrets about the Titans, the government, and humanity's history are revealed, leading to intense battles and philosophical questions about freedom and survival.

Tokyo revengers

Tokyo Revengers follows Takemichi Hanagaki, a 26-year-old who is devastated after learning that his ex-girlfriend Hinata and her brother were killed by the Tokyo Manji Gang. After an accident, Takemichi travels 12 years back in time to his teenage years. He discovers that he can alter the past, which could potentially save Hinata and change his future. Takemichi gets involved with violent gangs and must climb the ranks of the Tokyo Manji Gang while uncovering the reasons behind the tragic events, battling to protect his friends, and confronting his own weaknesses.

Kaiju No. 8

Kaiju No. 8 follows Kafka Hibino, a 32-year-old man who dreams of joining the Defense Force to combat giant monsters known as Kaiju. After an encounter with a Kaiju that gives him the ability to transform into one, Kafka becomes Kaiju No. 8. Balancing his new identity with his desire to protect humanity and fulfill his dream, Kafka must navigate his dual life while facing both monstrous threats and the challenges of the Defense Force. His journey blends action, personal growth, and the struggle to stay true to his principles in a world of chaos.
